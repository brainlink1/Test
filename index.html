Competition Timeline

Your journey from registration to victory

Registration Opens

Teams can register and submit their initial proposals. This is the first step to join the competition and showcase your innovative ideas.

Team Formation & Briefing

Participants form their cross-disciplinary teams and receive the competition brief with challenges from Henkel Algeria.

Solution Development

Teams work on developing their innovative solutions, combining business strategy with technical expertise.

Semi-Finals Submissions

Teams submit their final proposals for the semi-finals. Virtual presentations are presented to industry judges.

Grand Finale Selection

Top teams are selected to advance to the grand finale based on their semi-final performance and solution quality.

Final Presentation & Awards

The ultimate showdown at Henkel headquarters. Teams present their solutions to executive panels and winners are announced.
